# Factual Legal Status - PhoenixPME

✅ **Greg (@greg-gzillion) holds copyright** to original code written.

### What this means:
1. Copyright exists automatically upon creation
2. No registration required for basic protection
3. You control licensing of your original work
4. Others cannot claim your code as their own

### Limits:
- Contributors own copyright to their contributions
- GPL v3.0 grants specific rights to others
- Copyright doesn't prevent forks under GPL
